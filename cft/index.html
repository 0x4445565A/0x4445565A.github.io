Who

Three members of the Chickenfeet Travels Brood will be on the road creating memories for themselves and showing the world how enchanting Southeast Asia is. We have: J the mastermind and planner; M the eyes and visionary; and B the jack of all trades and king of code.

Why

Chickenfeet Travels is not just another travel company; we are a platform for local businesses who take social equality and environmental issues seriously. We are setting out on this motorbike mission to give voices to all the people we work with and show the world that sustainability always wins.

How

We are dedicating the next year to traveling around Southeast Asia on motorbikes to connect with new businesses, reconnect with old friends operating in all corners of this region and most importantly, tell their stories. A bonus is to capture photos and shoot videos that show the raw beauty of this region.

Our Patreon

Patreon is a monthly-based subscruption service that supports small projects and causes like Fly the Coop. You can become one of our patrons for as little as $1 a month up to $1000 and in exchange you will receive some awesome rewards.

A dollar goes a long way in Southeast Asia. That's a bowl of noodles, a full tank of petrol, and sometimes even a place to sleep for a night.

We will be travelling for the next year and the cost of travel adds up. We appreciate every dollar that comes in and if you are not in a place to, we are grateful for every share of our video and patreon page.

Our Plan

On motorbikes, we will be travelling from Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh on the longest route possible, detouring all the way through Laos, Thailand, and Cambodia. Then we will jump on planes (only because we have to) to start our Indonesia leg; travelling from Banda Aceh in North Sumatra all the way south and then east to Alor. We have our epic list of things to do: we will hit all major cities looking for businesses and organisations doing good work; we will meet new people making a difference in their community; and we will forge new partnerships and alliances with as many people we can find with the same ideals as Chickenfeet Travels. An environmental conscious, a heart for social equality, and a thirst to make a difference. And this isn't the end, future travels plans after this include Nepal, Sri Lanka, Myanmar, Philippines, and Borneo Island.

Sustainability

Chickenfeet Travels has a strong stance on environmental preservation and sustainability. As travellers, we often neglect how much waste we actually produce. We will travel with essential items that will eliminate the use of one-time-use containers, bags, straws and bottles. We will record everything we avoided using showing just how much damage the average person does. By making simple lifestyle changes, we will also be proving how easy it is to be more sustainable.
